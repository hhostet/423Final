A Clean You Can Feel


When you trust Happy Homes, you can count on Licensed, bonded, and insured professionals. Our flexible house cleaning services are designed to meet the needs of your unpredictable schedule. Although we suggest scheduling regular visits from our trained team members to maintain a consistently clean environment, we happily offer one-time cleaning services for when you’re expecting company, hosting a holiday get-together, or just in need of occasional house cleaning assistance. To ensure the best possible clean for those who enjoy regular cleaning visits, we follow a specific process every time. This extensive method is the foundation of how our room by room cleaning process works. Here's a breakdown of our standard cleaning procedures:


Basic Rooms: Living Rooms and Bedrooms

  • High Dust and Low Dust (Baseboards)

  • Dust/Wet wipe surfaces

  • Clean Up and Straighten
  • Vaccuum Floors and Upholstery

  • Clean Glass/Windows

  • Wet Mop Floors

Bathrooms

  • High Dust and Low Dust (Baseboards)

  • Scrub Toilets, Showers, and Sinks

  • Clean Up and Straighten
  • Vaccuum Floors and Upholstery

  • Clean Glass/Windows and Fixtures

  • Wet Mop Floors

Kitchens and Laundry Rooms

  • High Dust and Low Dust (Baseboards)

  • Dust/Wipe Surfaces, Furniture

  • Scrub and Wipe Out Sink
  • Vaccuum Floors and Upholstery

  • Clean Glass/Windows and Major Appliances

  • Wet Mop Floors

Other Included Features

  • 100% Non-Toxic and Green Cleaning Products

  • Bed Making and Fluffing

  • Blind and Vent Dusting
  • Door, Handle, and Light Switch Disinfectant

  • Spot Wall Washing
